Blueberry Cream Coffee Cake

The cream cheese makes all the difference in the texture. The streusel topping creates a great contrast. Enjoy the fruits of the season.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cups Butter
  • 1-¼ cup Sugar
  • 2 whole Eggs
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • ½ teaspoons Soda
  • ½ teaspoons Salt
  • ⅓ cups Milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 8 ounces, fluid Cream Cheese, Softened
  • 2 cups Blueberries
  • ½ cups Brown Sugar
  • ½ cups Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 3 Tablespoons Butter

Now let's get to the instructions:

  1. Cream 1/2 cup butter and 1 1/4 cup sugar; add eggs.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture.
  3. Add milk, vanilla, cream cheese. Stir well.
  4. Gently fold in blueberries. Spread in a greased 9 X 13 inch pan.
  5. Combine the 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1 tsp. cinnamon, 3 Tbsp. butter and 1/2 cup flour. Cut in butter until it resembles peas.
  6. Sprinkle over cake batter. Bake at 350 degrees for 40-50 minutes. Fabulous!
