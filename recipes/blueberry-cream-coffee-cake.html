Blueberry Cream Coffee Cake

The cream cheese makes all the difference in the texture. The streusel topping creates a great contrast. Enjoy the fruits of the season.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cups Butter
  • 1-¼ cup Sugar
  • 2 whole Eggs
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • ½ teaspoons Soda
  • ½ teaspoons Salt
  • ⅓ cups Milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 8 ounces, fluid Cream Cheese, Softened
  • 2 cups Blueberries
  • ½ cups Brown Sugar
  • ½ cups Flour
  • 1 teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 3 Tablespoons Butter
